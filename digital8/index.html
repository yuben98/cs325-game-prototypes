ZOMBIE SURVIVAL

  • Originality: This game is based on my Analog Assignment 5 which can be found on Bb. All the assets were custom made by myself, except for the sound effects. I fully made the player and zombie sprites from scratch which took me longer than I would've liked.
  • Technical Merit: I was able to incorporate many assets, play multiple sounds at different volumes, and make fully functionning and animated sprites. I have different sounds play for different events, in varying volumes. I incorporated some concepts from the textbook such as game feel. I had to work with many new elements: Instead of creating a new bullet every time the player shoots, which causes huge performance issues the longer the game goes on, I recycle the same bullet but have it stay invisible until needed. I also have a very basic AI for the zombies that follow and attack the player.
  • Prototype Postmortem: This is my second time converting an analog game into a digital one, and I learned many techniques that are bound to make the next sequence easier. I also already have some ideas planned for the next iteration of this game. I am more comfortable creating and animating a group of sprites and can easily work with bullets the next time around.
  • Prototype Assets: All graphical assets were custom made. The sound effects were borrowed from freesound.org.

  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This game is based on the Analog Assignment 5.
  • High Concept: Survive the zombie apocalypse. You have one gun.
  • Theme: There was no mandatory theme for this assignment.
  • Mandated Variety: Keyboard input; mouse input; both random and predetermined elements; competitor; adventure; overhead; infinite; shooter.
  • Prototype Goal: Kill the zombies and survive.
  • Player Experience Goals: The players will have fun and want to challenge themselves to reach a new highscore.
  • Gameplay: Use WASD to move. Mouse click to shoot. Shots can only go in the direction the player is facing, cannot aim with the mouse.
  • Strategies: Line up zombies and kill them.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: A man is stuck in a room with neverending spawning zombies.
  • Target Audience: Everyone.
  • Play Time: [0, infinity)
